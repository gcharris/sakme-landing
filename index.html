How Sakme works

1

Bring your material

Connect your notes, research, bookmarks, and drafts. Sakme indexes what you already have — it doesn't start from zero.

2

Create with your Alter Ego

Your AI collaborator learns your voice, your preferences, and your standards. It generates options. You decide what's good.

3

Every decision teaches it

When you choose, reject, or refine — Sakme learns. After weeks of use, it understands what you mean when you say "make it better."

4

Your understanding compounds

Insights from one project carry into the next. Your creative memory grows. The workspace becomes genuinely yours — not a blank page every time.

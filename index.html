How Sakme works

1

Bring your material

Connect your notes, research, bookmarks, and professional knowledge. Sakme indexes what you already have — it doesn't start from zero.

2

Create with your Alter Ego

Your AI collaborator learns your voice, your preferences, and your standards. It generates options. You decide what's good.

3

Every decision teaches it

When you choose, reject, or refine — Sakme learns. After weeks of use, it understands what you mean when you say “make it better.”

4

Your understanding compounds

Insights from one project carry into the next. Your professional memory grows. The workspace becomes genuinely yours — not a blank page every time.

5

Build what you need, sell what others need

The tools you build to solve your own problems can be published to the marketplace. Other professionals and creators buy them. You keep 100% of every sale.

6

Your workspace becomes your livelihood

Income tracking, expense tracking, and financial insights — built into the same workspace. The AI that knows your creative patterns also sees your business trajectory.
